Questions? We’ve Got Answers

Everything you need to know about our ERP, CRM, Website, and Mobile App solutions.

What does your ERP solution include?

AppVerge supports easy integration with Google Analytics, Plausible, and other tracking tools. Just paste your tracking ID, and we’ll handle the setup for seamless data collection.

How does your CRM help manage customers?

Our CRM gives you a 360° view of every customer — track leads, manage pipelines, automate follow-ups, and analyse sales performance. It integrates seamlessly with your website and mobile app for real-time data sync.

What kind of websites do you build?

We design and develop all types of websites — corporate portals, e-commerce stores, landing pages, and service platforms. Every website is responsive, SEO-optimized, and comes with cloud hosting and SSL included.

Do you develop apps for both Android and iOS?

Yes. We build cross-platform mobile apps for both Android and iOS. Whether you need a customer-facing app or an internal business tool, we deliver high-performance apps tailored to your requirements.

Can ERP, CRM, website, and mobile app be integrated together?

Absolutely. Our solutions are built to work together seamlessly. Your website, mobile app, CRM, and ERP all share a common data layer — giving you a unified view of your business operations in real time.

What support do you provide after delivery?

We provide dedicated post-delivery support including bug fixes, updates, and feature enhancements. Our team ensures your ERP, CRM, website, and mobile app continue running smoothly as your business evolves.
